# YuristAI FAQ
_Last updated: [02.06.2026]_

## 1. What is YuristAI?
YuristAI is an AI chatbot that helps you find, read, summarize, and better understand laws and legal documents available on lex.uz and other public/official sources.

## 2. Is YuristAI an official government service?
No. YuristAI is an independent app and is not a government authority.

## 3. Is YuristAI affiliated with lex.uz?
No. YuristAI is not affiliated with lex.uz unless we clearly state otherwise.

## 4. Does YuristAI provide legal advice?
No. YuristAI provides informational help only. It does not replace a qualified lawyer and does not create a lawyer–client relationship.

## 5. How should I use YuristAI safely?
Use YuristAI to learn and navigate documents, but always verify important details in the official text and consult a lawyer for decisions with legal consequences (deadlines, court, contracts, disputes).

## 6. What sources does YuristAI use?
YuristAI may reference publicly available legal texts and documents from lex.uz and other official/public sources. We may provide citations or links when available.

## 7. Can YuristAI show the original document text?
YuristAI may show excerpts, summaries, or references. When possible, it will provide a link so you can open the original on lex.uz and read the official version.

## 8. Why can AI answers be wrong?
AI can misunderstand context, miss updates, or summarize incorrectly. Laws can also change. Always confirm with the official document text.

## 9. What should I do if I find an incorrect answer?
Please report it. If your app has a “Report” button, use it. Otherwise email us with:
- The question you asked
- The answer you received
- A link to the correct lex.uz document (if you have it)

## 10. Can I upload my own documents?
If the app supports it, you can upload or paste text only if you have the right to use it. Avoid sending sensitive personal data (passport numbers, bank details, private medical info).

## 11. Does YuristAI store my chats?
Depending on the app version and settings, some chat data may be stored to improve quality, ensure security, and troubleshoot issues. Please check the Privacy Policy inside the app for details.

## 12. Can I use YuristAI offline?
Some features may work offline (for example, viewing cached content), but AI answers and up-to-date documents generally require an internet connection.

## 13. Which languages are supported?
YuristAI supports multiple languages, including Uzbek (Latin), Uzbek (Cyrillic), Russian, and English (availability may depend on your app version).

## 14. How do I change the app language?
Go to Settings in the app and select your preferred language (if available). Some devices may also control language via system settings.

## 15. Is YuristAI free?
Pricing depends on your app version and region. If a subscription or paid plan exists, the app will show it clearly.
